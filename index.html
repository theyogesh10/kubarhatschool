Welcome to Government Higher Secondary School Kubarhat

We are proud to serve the children of our rural community in Kubarhat, Kundam block of Jabalpur district, Madhya Pradesh. Run by the Department of Education, our co‑educational institution follows the state curriculum and provides primary and elementary learning with Hindi as the medium of instruction. We believe in creating a positive and supportive environment where every student can grow with confidence, learn new skills and participate actively in both academics and activities that strengthen character and community values.
