About GHSS Kubarhat

Government Higher Secondary School, Kubarhat, Jabalpur, is a rural government‑run educational institution located in the village of Kubarhat in the Kundam block of Jabalpur district, Madhya Pradesh. Managed by the Department of Education, the school provides co‑educational learning with Hindi as the medium of instruction and follows the state board curriculum from primary classes onwards. It aims to make education accessible to local children and encourage regular attendance, participation and overall development. With basic facilities such as classrooms, a library, playground and a supportive group of teachers, the school strives to nurture a strong foundation of knowledge and life skills, preparing students for future academic opportunities and personal growth.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution that nurtures academic excellence, creativity, and social responsibility.

Our Mission

  • Provide quality education with modern teaching methods
  • Foster critical thinking and creativity
  • Encourage sports, arts, and extracurricular activities
  • Support students’ overall development and well-being

Achievements

  • Top performers in state-level competitions
  • Active participation in cultural and sports events
